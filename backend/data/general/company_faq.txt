FOODCHOW - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I submit a reimbursement claim?
A: Log into the HR portal at hr.foodchow.com, navigate to Finance > Reimbursements, fill out the form, attach receipts, and submit. Claims are processed within 5 business days. You must submit within 30 days of the expense.

Q: What are the office hours?
A: Standard office hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday. The office is accessible 24/7 with your employee badge for those who need to work outside standard hours.

Q: How many days of annual leave do I get?
A: Full-time employees receive 20 days of annual leave per year, accrued at 1.67 days per month. Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.

Q: How do I request work from home?
A: Submit WFH requests through the HR portal by Thursday for the following week. You can work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Q: When is the next company all-hands meeting?
A: All-hands meetings are held quarterly. The next all-hands is scheduled for October 15, 2025 at 11 AM in the main auditorium and streamed live for remote employees.

Q: How do I raise an IT support ticket?
A: Email helpdesk@foodchow.com with a description of your issue. For urgent issues, call ext. 911. Response times are 4 hours for critical issues and 24 hours for standard requests.

Q: What is the professional development budget?
A: Each employee has a $1,500 annual budget for professional development including courses, certifications, books, and conferences. Submit requests through the HR portal for approval.

Q: How do I update my personal information?
A: Log into the HR portal and navigate to My Profile > Personal Information. Changes to banking details require verification through HR directly.

Q: What health insurance plans are available?
A: FoodChow offers three plans: Basic, Standard, and Premium. Details are available on the HR portal under Benefits. Open enrollment is in November each year.

Q: How do I refer someone for a job opening?
A: Submit referrals through the HR portal under Careers > Employee Referral. Successful referrals that result in a hire receive a $2,000 bonus after the new hire completes 90 days.
