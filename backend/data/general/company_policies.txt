FOODCHOW - COMPANY POLICIES AND EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

LEAVE POLICY
Annual Leave: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)
Sick Leave: 12 days per year
Casual Leave: 6 days per year
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks fully paid
Paternity Leave: 4 weeks fully paid
Bereavement Leave: 5 days
Leave carry-forward: Maximum 10 days to next year

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week
Core hours (mandatory online): 10 AM - 4 PM local time
WFH requests must be submitted by Thursday for the following week
Employees must have stable internet (minimum 20 Mbps)
Video must be on during team meetings

WORKING HOURS
Standard Hours: 9 AM - 6 PM (Monday to Friday)
Flexible Start: Between 8 AM - 10 AM (with manager approval)
Overtime: Compensated at 1.5x rate for hours beyond 45/week
Weekend work requires prior written manager approval

REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
Internet allowance: $50/month for WFH employees
Home office setup: One-time $500 reimbursement
Professional development: Up to $1,500/year per employee
Travel: Economy class for flights under 6 hours
Meals during travel: Up to $75/day
Submit reimbursements within 30 days of expense

IT SUPPORT
Submit IT tickets at: helpdesk@foodchow.com
Response time SLA: 4 hours for critical, 24 hours for standard
Emergency IT support: ext. 911 or it-emergency@foodchow.com
